Honey Chicken!

Description

This meal is perfect as a meal prep, or to be used
in other recipes. Can be used as a protein source for
bowls, burritos, stir-frys, and more!

If you are interested in more recipes just like this one,
then please look through the rest of my page for more!

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds skinless chicken breast
  • Salt
  • Garlic powder
  • Onion powder
  • Smoked Paprika
  • 7-8 Chipotle Peppers, minced
  • 4 tablespoons Adobo sauce
  • 30 g Worcestershire sauce
  • 60g Hot Honey
  • 10g Dijon mustard

Steps

  1. Add minced peppers, adobo sauce, Worcestershire,
    hot honey, dijon mustard, seasonings,
    and salt to a large bowl. Mix until smooth.
  2. Add boneless chicken to the marinade and let sit for 1-2 hours
  3. When ready to cook, preheat to broil at 475
  4. Oil baking sheet, add chicken to sheet, and cook for 12 minutes
  5. Let rest for 5-10 minutes. Save in fridge for up to a week or eat right there!
