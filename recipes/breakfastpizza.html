Breakfast Pizza!

Description

This breakfast pizza is done in under 30 minutes
and would be perfect for an addition to brunch.

Ingredients

  • 3 sausage patties
  • 1 large tortilla
  • 1/2 cup shredded cheese
  • 1/3 cup salsa
  • 1 egg
  • Cilantro
  • Black Pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Crumble sausage into a large skillet and cook until browned over medium-high heat,
    about 5 minutes. Drain grease and remove browned pork
    to a plate. Clean out skillet.
  3. Place flour tortilla into the skillet.
    Sprinkle 1/2 of the cheese onto the tortilla
    and spread salsa over the cheese.
    Top with sausage crumbles and remaining cheese.
  4. Crack open the egg and carefully add to the middle of the pizza;
    cook for 12 minutes.
  5. Top pizza with avocado slices,
    chopped cilantro, sour cream (if preferred), and black pepper.
